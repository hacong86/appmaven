FAQ

Can I add my logo to the QR code?
Yes! QRCreator allows you to overlay custom logos or images on your QR codes to make them stand out and match your brand identity.
Does it support Wi-Fi codes?
Absolutely. You can create a QR code containing your Wi-Fi credentials. Guests just scan it to connect instantly—no typing passwords required.
Is my data secure?
Your privacy is our priority. QR code data is generally stored locally on your device. We do not require account registration to use the core features.

The Ultimate Tool for the QR Age

From contactless menus to digital business cards, QR codes are everywhere. QRCreator puts the power to create and read them in your pocket. It's not just a utility; it's a productivity booster for professionals and everyday users alike.

  • Marketing: Direct customers to your website or social media.
