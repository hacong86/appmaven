About this game

Square Race Max drops dozens of smart little squares into a physics-driven arena. They dash, collide, grab weapons and outplay rivals until only one square—or none—remains.

Pick from 25 handcrafted maps, relax in Arena Mode, or unleash your creativity in Editor Mode to build and share your own battlegrounds.

FAQ

Is it multiplayer?
Square Race MAX focuses on single-player offline experience where you can simulate battles between autonomous squares or build your own arenas.
How does the Editor work?
Editor Mode allows you to paint terrain, drop pickups, and place deadly traps. Once your map is ready, simply press Play to see squares fight on your custom battleground.
Is it free?
Yes, Square Race MAX is free to download and play. We offer optional in-app purchases for premium content, but the core game and editor are accessible to everyone.
Does it need internet?
No, Square Race MAX is fully playable offline. You can build maps and run simulations anywhere without an internet connection.
