Why ChemLab is the Essential Chemistry App in 2026

In the world of science education, having the right tools can make all the difference. ChemLab: Periodic Table is designed to be the ultimate companion for students, researchers, and professional chemists alike. By combining a rich, interactive periodic table with seven specialized calculation tools, we've created a laboratory suite that fits right in your pocket.

Master Chemical Equations and Stoichiometry

Solving chemistry homework can be time-consuming. Our advanced chemical equation balancer takes the guesswork out of stoichiometry. Simply enter your reactants and products, and get the balanced equation instantly. Whether you are dealing with simple synthesis or complex redox reactions, ChemLab provides the correct coefficient every time.

Comprehensive Element Data for Research

Need to find the melting point of Tungsten or the orbital configuration of Xenon? ChemLab offers an exhaustive database for all 118 elements. Explore isotopes, electronegativity, atomic radius, and more. Our interactive periodic table is color-coded and highly searchable, helping you find exactly what you need for your lab reports or exams in seconds.

Privacy-First Academic Tool

We believe that academic tools should be fast and unintrusive. That's why ChemLab works completely offline. Your calculations and research history are stored locally on your device, ensuring total privacy. No accounts, no data collection—just pure chemistry tools when and where you need them.
