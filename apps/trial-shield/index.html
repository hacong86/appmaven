FAQ

Do you connect to my bank?
No. Trial Shield is a manual tracker. We do not ask for banking credentials or read your SMS. This ensures 100% privacy and security for your financial data.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device's internal database. You don't need an internet connection to view or edit your subscriptions.
What is the Biometric Lock?
This is a Premium feature that allows you to lock the app using your phone's fingerprint or face unlock, adding an extra layer of security to your data.
Can I backup my data?
Yes, you can export your entire subscription list to a JSON file for backup and import it later or on another device.

Why Trial Shield is the Must-Have Subscription Manager

In the subscription economy, it is easy to lose track of where your money is going. Trial Shield creates clarity from the chaos. As dedicated subscription manager app, it helps you consolidate all your recurring payments—streaming services, cloud storage, fitness apps—into one clean, dark-mode dashboard.

The Ultimate Free Trial Tracker

We've all been there: signing up for a "free 7-day trial" and forgetting to cancel, only to be charged $50 a week later. Trial Shield is your personal cancel free trial reminder. When you add a trial, our system automatically schedules robust alerts: one warning a few days before, and a "Final Call" on the last day. Save hundreds of dollars a year by stopping unexpected charges before they happen.

Privacy-First: No Banks, No Spying

Most finance apps want your bank login or access to read your SMS messages. We don't. Trial Shield is built on a Privacy First architecture. We are an offline subscription tracker; your data lives in a secure Sandbox on your phone and never touches our servers. This makes it the perfect tool for privacy-conscious users who want to manage their budget without exposing their financial life.

Reliable Alerts on Android 12-15

Notifications on modern Android can be tricky due to battery optimization. Trial Shield utilizes a custom hybrid WorkManager + AlarmManager system designed explicitly for Android 12 through 15. This ensures your renewal alerts arrive exactly at 9:00 AM, every time. We even include a persistence check to ensure you never miss a bill.

Understand Your Digital Spending

Knowledge is power. Trial Shield provides a powerful spend overview that instantly calculates your total monthly and yearly liability. View your spending separated by currency without messy conversions. Whether you are a student managing a tight budget or a freelancer tracking software costs, this digital spending manager gives you the financial visibility you need.
