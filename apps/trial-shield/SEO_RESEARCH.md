# SEO Strategy for Trial Shield

Based on the app's focus on privacy, subscription management, and free trial tracking, here is the tailored keyword research.

## 🎯 Primary Keywords (High Intent)
> Target in H1, Title, and SEO Content blocks

- **Subscription Manager App** (Broad category)
- **Free Trial Tracker** (Specific high-pain point)
- **Cancel Free Trial Reminder** (Action-oriented)
- **Privacy First Finance App** (Niche audience)
- **Offline Subscription Tracker** (Technical differentiator)

## 🚀 "Blue Ocean" Keywords (Low Competition)
> Use in Feature lists and detailed descriptions

- **"Track renewals without bank connection"** (Privacy concern)
- **"Avoid subscription traps"** (Problem-aware users)
- **"Digital spending overview"** (Budgeting angle)
- **"Biometric locked finance app"** (Security angle)
- **"Android 15 subscription reminders"** (Tech-specific)

## 📝 SEO Content Optimization Plan

### 1. Meta Title
`Trial Shield: Subscription Manager & Free Trial Tracker | Privacy First`
*Why:* Covers main function (Manager) and key hook (Free Trial) + Brand value (Privacy).

### 2. Meta Description
`Take control of your digital spending with Trial Shield. The offline subscription manager that tracks renewals and sends free trial alerts. No bank connection required.`

### 3. URL Structure
`/apps/trial-shield/`

### 4. Image Alt Text Strategy
- "Trial Shield subscription dashboard dark mode"
- "Free trial reminder notification setup"
- "Monthly digital spending summary"

## 📊 Keyword Integration Checklist for `index.html`

- [x] **H1:** Trial Shield: Subscription Manager
- [x] **H2:** Why Trial Shield? (Target: "Privacy-first", "Offline")
- [x] **Content:** Mention "unexpected charges", "data privacy", "local storage".
