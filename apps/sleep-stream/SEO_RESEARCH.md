# SEO Strategy for Sleep Stream

Based on the app's focus on sleep sounds, relaxation, and offline functionality, here is the tailored keyword research.

## 🎯 Primary Keywords (High Intent)
> Target in H1, Title, and SEO Content blocks

- **Sleep Needs App** (Broad category)
- **White Noise Machine** (High search volume)
- **Offline Sleep Sounds** (Specific technical constraint)
- **Rain Sounds for Sleeping** (Specific content search)
- **Focus Sound App** (Secondary use case)

## 🚀 "Blue Ocean" Keywords (Low Competition)
> Use in Feature lists and detailed descriptions

- **"Mix your own sleep sounds"** (Feature specific)
- **"No subscription relaxation app"** (Monetization pain point)
- **"Brown noise for ADHD"** (Specific noise color trend)
- **"Sleep timer with fade out"** (Feature specific)
- **"Offline meditation sounds"** (Use case)

## 📝 SEO Content Optimization Plan

### 1. Meta Title
`Sleep Stream: Relax & Sleep Sounds | White Noise & Rain | Offline`
*Why:* Covers main function (Relax & Sleep), specific content (White Noise, Rain), and key feature (Offline).

### 2. Meta Description
`Fall asleep faster with Sleep Stream. Mix rain, ocean, and white noise to create your perfect offline soundscape. No login, no ads, just relaxation.`

### 3. URL Structure
`/apps/sleep-stream/`

### 4. Image Alt Text Strategy
- "Sleep Stream sound mixer interface"
- "Dark mode sleep timer settings"
- "Rain and thunder sound selection"

- [x] **Content:** Mention "insomnia", "tinnitus relief", "deep work", "baby sleep".
