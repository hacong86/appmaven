FAQ

How does the prompt builder work?
Our builder uses dynamic variables placeholders. You select a template, fill in the blanks (like [Topic] or [Tone]), and copy the finalized prompt instantly.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your custom prompts and history are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your creative content to any cloud servers.
Which AIs are supported?
The text prompts work universally with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others. The image prompts are optimized for Midjourney v5/v6, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion.
Is it free?
The app is free to download with hundreds of free prompts. The PRO version unlocks the full library of 980+ prompts and advanced styling features.
