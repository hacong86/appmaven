Trust nothing. Jump anyway.

A rage platformer built to break your patience

Trap Tower turns a simple jump into a mind game. Fake floors crumble the moment you land, doors run away, spikes rain from the ceiling, and saws wait in the dark. This is a rage game — you will die, a lot, and that is the whole point.

Every death teaches you the next trick. Memorize the trap, time the jump, and inch your way up a tower that is actively laughing at you. Controls stay tight and fair, so every mistake is yours to fix — never the game's fault.

FAQ

Trap Tower questions

What kind of game is Trap Tower?

It is a troll rage platformer. You climb 200 hand-crafted levels packed with 50 deadly traps, where every floor, door, and spike can be a lie waiting to spring.

Where can I download Trap Tower?

Trap Tower is available now on Google Play for Android. An iOS version is coming soon.

Does Trap Tower require an account?

No account is required. Your progress, unlocked levels, and death count are saved locally on your device, and the game never asks for your name, email, or phone number.

Does Trap Tower have ads or in-app purchases?

It is free to play with Google AdMob ads — interstitial ads between levels and an optional rewarded ad to skip a hard level. There are currently no in-app purchases.

Can I play with a keyboard or on PC?

Yes. Trap Tower supports a touch joystick and jump button on phones, plus full keyboard controls (Arrows, WASD, Space) on Chromebook and PC.

Is Trap Tower really that hard?

Yes — that is the point. The traps are mean, but the controls are fair, so every death is a lesson and every clear is earned.

Dare to climb?

The tower is laughing at you. Download Trap Tower on Android and wipe that smirk off its face — 200 levels and 50 traps are waiting.
