FAQ

Why only one task?
Radical single-tasking removes decision fatigue. By committing to just one major goal, you ensure meaningful progress every single day.
Can I edit my goal?
No. FocusOne uses a "Commitment Lock". Once you set today's goal, there's no edit button. You must finish it or face a broken streak. This forces you to be intentional.
Is it good for ADHD?
Yes! Many users with ADHD find massive to-do lists overwhelming. FocusOne's minimalist approach reduces anxiety by focusing on just the immediate next step.
Is it free?
FocusOne is free to download and use. We believe productivity should be accessible to everyone who wants to improve their focus.

Why FocusOne is the Best Minimalist Daily Planner in 2026

In an era of endless notifications and complex project management tools, FocusOne stands out by doing less, so you can achieve more. As a premier daily focus planner, it strips away the noise of traditional to-do lists, helping you master the art of single-tasking. It is not just an app; it is a philosophy designed to combat burnout and decision fatigue.

The Ultimate ADHD Friendly Planner

For neurodivergent brains, a long list of tasks can be paralyzing—a phenomenon known as "wall of awful." FocusOne is designed as an ADHD friendly planner that removes this friction. By forcing you to choose only one priority, it provides clear executive direction. The interface is high-contrast, distraction-free, and intentionally limited in functionality to prevent "procrasti-planning" (spending hours organizing tasks instead of doing them).

Overcoming Procrastination with Commitment

What makes FocusOne unique is its "Commitment Lock" feature. Unlike other to-do list apps where you can endlessly reschedule tasks, FocusOne requires you to commit. This psychological trigger shifts your mindset from "I might do this" to "I must do this." It is the perfect procrastination helper for students, freelancers, and anyone struggling to self-motivate.

Build Habits with Streak Motivation

Consistency is key to success. FocusOne includes a built-in habit tracker streak system inspired by the "Don't Break the Chain" method. Seeing your visual progress daily releases dopamine and encourages you to show up, even on hard days. Unlock daily inspiration quotes as rewards for your discipline, turning your daily productivity into a satisfying game.

Deep Work Tool for Radical Focus

In a distracted world, the ability to perform deep work is a superpower. FocusOne acts as your personal intent shield. By having your one daily goal clearly visible, you can easily re-orient yourself whenever you get distracted. Whether you are writing a book, coding a feature, or studying for exams, FocusOne is the essential deep work tool to keep you on track.
