3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information collected automatically by advertising and analytics SDKs

The Game shows advertising and measures gameplay through third-party software development kits (SDKs). Through these SDKs, the following may be collected:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID): a resettable identifier provided by your device, used to deliver and measure advertising. In regions where consent is required, personalized advertising is used only if you consent.
  • Firebase installation / app instance ID: a non-account identifier used to group analytics events from the same app installation.
  • Device and technical information: device model, operating system version, language, country/region, and network connection state.
  • Approximate location: inferred from your IP address at the country/region level. We do not collect GPS or precise location.
  • Gameplay and interaction events: starting or finishing a run, leveling up, choosing upgrades, summoning pets, selecting heroes and maps, ad interactions, and purchase interactions.
  • Attribution / install source: via the Google Play Install Referrer, used to understand where installs come from.

3.2 Information related to in-app purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases, such as gem packs, "Remove Ads", and "Starter Pack". Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation of the purchase so we can deliver the item. Purchase events, including product identifier and price paid as reported by the store, are logged to Google Analytics for Firebase and to Meta (Facebook App Events) for revenue analytics and advertising measurement. We do not receive or store your payment card or billing details.

3.3 Information stored only on your device

Your game progress, including in-game currencies, unlocked heroes, pets and maps, permanent upgrades, quests and daily-login progress, and the "Remove Ads" flag, is saved as a local file in the Game's private storage on your device. This data stays on your device, is not uploaded to us, and is deleted when you uninstall the Game or clear its data.

If your device has Google Auto Backup enabled, this local file may be backed up to your own Google account per your device settings. We have no access to those backups.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Display advertising, including rewarded ads you choose to watch, and measure ad performance and prevent ad fraud;
  • Understand how players use the Game and improve gameplay, balance and stability;
  • Process and deliver your in-app purchases and restore non-consumable purchases such as "Remove Ads";
  • Diagnose crashes and technical problems. Crash logs are stored locally on your device and are not automatically uploaded to us;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell your personal data for money.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent for personalized advertising and the use of your Advertising ID for that purpose.
  • Legitimate interests for analytics, non-personalized advertising, fraud prevention, and improving and securing the Game.
  • Performance of a contract to process and deliver the in-app purchases you request.
  • Legal obligation where we must process data to comply with the law.

6. Third-Party Services and SDKs

The Game integrates the following third-party services, which may act as independent data controllers or processors and collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) serves banner, interstitial and rewarded advertising, and processes your Advertising ID and ad-interaction data. How Google uses data and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) presents the consent form used to gather your advertising choices in regions where consent is required.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase provides gameplay analytics and collects an app instance identifier, device/technical information and usage events. Firebase and privacy.
  • Google Play Billing processes in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Platforms (Facebook SDK) is used solely for advertising measurement, including app installs and in-app events such as level completion and purchases, so we can run and optimize install campaigns on Facebook and Instagram. The Game does not include Facebook Login and we receive no Facebook profile information. Meta Privacy Policy.
  • Unity (Unity Technologies) and Unity Gaming Services are used to process in-app purchases through Unity IAP. Unity Privacy Policy.

These partners may use device identifiers and similar technologies, including Google's Privacy Sandbox features on supported Android versions, to deliver and measure advertising.

7. Advertising, Personalized Ads, and Your Choices

In the EEA, the UK and Switzerland, the first time you open the Game we present a consent form via Google's User Messaging Platform asking whether you agree to the use of your data for personalized advertising. Ads are personalized only if you consent; otherwise you receive non-personalized ads where available.

You can change or withdraw your choice at any time from the Privacy Options button in the Game's Settings menu.

You can also control ad personalization at the device level:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization" and/or "Reset advertising ID" or "Delete advertising ID".

Opting out means you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your advertising identifier.
