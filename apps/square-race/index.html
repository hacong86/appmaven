FAQ

Do I control the squares?
No! Square Race is a "hands-off" physics simulator. You set up the simulation or choose a map, and the smart squares run autonomously. You are the observer of the chaos.
What can I do in Editor Mode?
You can place walls, death traps, speed boosters, teleport gates, and more. Design a maze or an open killing field, then hit play to test it out.
What are Clone Boosters?
Clone Boosters multiply any square that passes through them, creating swarms of duplicates. This adds a layer of unpredictability and massive scale to the battles.
Is it offline?
Yes, the game is fully playable offline. Perfect for short bursts or long chill sessions without needing Wi-Fi.

Why You’ll Love Square Race

Sometimes you don't want to sweat over complex controls. Square Race delivers endless replay value through observation and experimentation. Whether you observe chaotic physics battles or fine-tune map layouts to craft nail-biting showdowns, it's satisfaction guaranteed.

Arena Mode: Chaos Unfolded

Choose from a variety of built-in maps and observe how your Squares interact with:

  • Death Wall: Relentlessly advances and shrinks the safe area.
  • Breakable Walls: Crumble on impact, dynamic pathfinding.
  • Teleport Gates: Instant relocation across the arena.
  • Speed Boosters: Turbo-charge passing Squares.
  • Clone Boosters: Multiply Squares into swarming duplicates.

Editor Mode: Unleash Creativity

Don't just watch—create. The robust Level Editor lets you:

  • Place Walls & Hazards exactly where you want them.
  • Add Boosters to craft unique challenges.
  • Run Simulations immediately, Save and replay your creations anytime.
