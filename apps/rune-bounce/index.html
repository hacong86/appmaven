Aim. Bounce. Defend.

A spell-bouncing defense game built for quick, tactical runs

Rune Bounce turns brick breaker aiming into a fantasy defense battle. Instead of clearing ordinary blocks, you launch magic shots through enemy formations, bounce them off walls, trigger elemental effects, and protect the gate before the darkness breaks through.

The game is designed for players who like arcade precision with RPG decisions. Each run asks you to read the board, plan a ricochet path, pick upgrades, and build a wizard that can survive stronger waves and bosses.

Progression

From one clean shot to a stronger wizard build

Rune Bounce is built around the satisfying moment when a single spell rebound clears the board, but it also gives each run a reason to matter. Collect resources, improve equipment, and return with a stronger setup for harder waves.

The best runs are not only about raw power. They come from reading the field, choosing upgrades that fit the enemies ahead, and using the right angle at the right moment.

FAQ

Rune Bounce questions

What kind of game is Rune Bounce: Magic Defense?

It is a magic brick breaker roguelite RPG. You aim spell shots, bounce them through enemies and blocks, choose run upgrades, collect gear, and defend the gate.

Where can I download Rune Bounce?

Rune Bounce is available on Google Play for Android devices through the official AppMaven Studio listing.

Does Rune Bounce require an account?

No account is required. The game saves progress locally on your device and does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or address.

Does Rune Bounce include ads or in-app purchases?

Yes. The game uses Google AdMob for rewarded and interstitial ads, and Google Play Billing for purchases such as virtual currency, remove ads options, and progression-related offers.
