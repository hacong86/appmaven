Specific data practices vary by app. Each app has a dedicated privacy policy accessible via its Google Play Store listing. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

You can request data deletion using our Data Deletion Request Form. We'll process your request within 30 days. For most apps, simply uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data from your device.

Account & Purchases

In-app purchases are tied to your Google Play account. If you reinstall an app or install it on a new device:

  1. Open the app
  2. Sign in with the same Google account you used to make the purchase
  3. Your purchases should automatically restore

If purchases don't restore, look for a "Restore Purchases" button in the app's settings or contact us for assistance.

Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Google Play, not directly by us. To request a refund:

  1. Visit Google Play Order History
  2. Find the purchase you want to refund
  3. Click "Request a refund" or "Report a problem"

Refunds are typically available within 48 hours of purchase. After that, contact Google Play Support or email us if you need assistance.

Subscriptions are managed through Google Play:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap Menu (☰) → Subscriptions
  3. Select the subscription you want to cancel
  4. Tap "Cancel subscription"

You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Note: Uninstalling the app does NOT cancel your subscription.
