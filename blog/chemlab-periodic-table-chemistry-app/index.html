Why Chemistry Students Need ChemLab

Chemistry is one of the most tool-heavy subjects in STEM. Students regularly need to balance equations, calculate molar masses, look up element data, find solubility, and predict reaction products — often all within a single study session or lab period.

Most chemistry apps on the market give you one thing: a periodic table. ChemLab gives you everything. It's the difference between carrying a ruler and carrying a full engineering toolkit.

"Built on Zperiod — a trusted web chemistry platform used by thousands of students — ChemLab brings the same accuracy and depth to your mobile device."

What makes ChemLab truly unique is its commitment to mathematical accuracy. Every tool — from the equation balancer to the empirical formula calculator — uses verified algorithms, not approximations. You're not getting "close enough." You're getting chemistry done right.

All 7 Chemistry Tools — Reviewed

🔬

Interactive Periodic Table

All 118 elements with 4 levels of detail: basic properties, atomic structure, electronic configuration, and discovery history. Features pinch-to-zoom, landscape wide-view, and an Element of the Day.

⚖️

Chemical Equation Balancer

Auto-balances any equation using matrix-based RREF math. Supports hydrates (CuSO₄·5H₂O), state symbols, and complex multi-element equations. Always mathematically correct.

⚗️

Molar Mass Calculator

Full element-by-element breakdown including count, atomic mass, subtotal, and percentage contribution. Supports nested formulas like Ca(OH)₂ and Al₂(SO₄)₃.

⚡

Ion Engine

40+ ions with comprehensive detail pages. Covers monatomic, polyatomic, and transition metal ions. Color-coded by group with fast search and filtering.

💧

Solubility Checker

Instant lookup: Soluble, Insoluble, or Slightly Soluble. Beginner Guided mode and Expert mode. Covers all standard solubility rules including nitrates, sulfates, and carbonates.

🧮

Empirical & Molecular Formula

Convert mass percentages to simplest formula, then find the actual molecular formula from empirical data + molar mass. Step-by-step ratio display for learning, not just answers.

🔮 Reaction Predictor

The Reaction Predictor is one of ChemLab's most impressive features. Select a reaction type — Synthesis, Decomposition, Single Replacement, Double Replacement, or Combustion — and ChemLab predicts the products using activity series, halogen reactivity, and solubility-driven rules. Results come with color-coded cards and plain-English explanations so you actually understand what's happening, not just see an answer.

📝 Worksheet Generator

Uniquely useful for teachers and serious students, the Worksheet Generator creates fresh chemistry practice problems on demand. Choose your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard) and question type (Equation Balancing, Molar Mass Calculation), then export to PDF for printing or offline study. It's a lesson planning tool built right into the app.
